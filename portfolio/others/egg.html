PRESENT FOR AN EMPRESS

Hi folks, this is one of the most intricate project I worked on throughout this year at school. It was done strictly on my free time and was considered as a training in several field of expertise. This project appealed to various skills, such as modeling, texturing, lighting, rendering, and others. I also had the chance to practice technical skills such as the NCloth tools in Maya for instance or some very advanced curve modeling. Overall, this project was truly initiatory and quite a great experience for me. The result of this project and it's overall quality is due mostly to the support of my family and of one of my closest friends without whom this project wouldn't even have had existed.
