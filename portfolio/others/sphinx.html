EGYPTIAN SPHINX STATUE

Some renders of a sphinx statue I did on a Zbrush sculpture module in class... The project was done only on my free time with a five weeks deadline. It was my first ever big sculpt project, and overall I'm kinda happy and most of all proud of the quality of the renders that I did for this project... The renders where done inside Maya with Pixar's Photorealistic Renderman... I find the way that Renderman works very awesome, and in the following weeks and months I will try to get better at it... But for that to happen you'll have to wait a little longer
