WALK THROUGH THE RUINS OF LINE 13 AND JOIN THE REMAINS OF HUMANITY.

Ligne 13 is a survival game which brings you on a parisian-post apocalyptic metropolitan. Harvest several type of ressources to grow your arsenal. Grow elemental seeds to help you break through various obstacles. Survives hordes of mutant enemies. Try your best to join the remaining of humanity
