NAUKAR

Second Year student project | 2021

Explore the depths of this misterious cave in search for your wizard master !

Naukar is an action adventure roguelite game. The game is a solo adventure playable on Windows PCs only with mouse and keyboard. Naukar is actually in its final development state and playable, however, when playing, you'll have to remember that the game was procudes over only 8 weeks of production with a small team of students, so please be clement with every bugs you may encounter.
